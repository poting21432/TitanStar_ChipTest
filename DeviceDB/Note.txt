Migration:產生連結db檔的程式碼
dotnet ef migrations add InitialCreate --project .\Pyramid.Data.SQLite
移除Migration
ef migrations remove
生成db檔
dotnet ef database update --project .\Pyramid.Data.SQLite
生成Entity Source
Scaffold-DbContext "Data Source=data.db" Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.Sqlite -OutputDir Models
Scaffold-DbContext "Data Source=configs.db" Microsoft.EntityFrameworkCore.Sqlite -OutputDir Configs
